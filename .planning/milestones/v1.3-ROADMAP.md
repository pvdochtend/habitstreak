**Key Decisions:**

- D-10-01: Use "purpose: any" instead of "any maskable" (icon design not maskable-safe)
- D-11-01: Server-side auth check with redirect (simpler than route groups)
- D-11-02: Stylized mockup instead of screenshot (consistent across themes)
- D-12-01: Reusable AuthHeader component (consistent branding across auth pages)

**Issues Resolved:**
